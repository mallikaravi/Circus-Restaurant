About Us

Welcome to Circus Restaurant, your #1 place for tasty food and interesting circus shows. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of environment , with a focus on three characteristics, ie: affordablity, best customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2023 by Lexicon Team Group-1, Circus restaurant has started its beginnings in a heart of Europe's capital Stockholm. When a team of 5 amazing personalities named Yuran, Cristine, Imran, Mallika, and Kantha started to work on their passion for helping the people by introducing the best place to enjoy within Stockholm. They did an intense research to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming Circus Restaurant. We hope you will enjoy your time at Circus Restaurant as much as we enjoy surving you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
